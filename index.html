Timeline

How It All Started | Fall, 2009
During my first quarter of attending UCSB, I volunteered to represent my dorm at the residence Hall Council. I became hooked on being active in my community.

Co-Founded Phi Sigma Pi | Winter, 2010
I co-founded the Zeta Epsilon chapter of Phi Sigma Pi National Co-ed Honors Fraternity with some amazing people and served on the executive board since then.

Elected on E-board | Fall, 2010
During my sophomore year I lived in the dorms again and was elected as an executive board member of Hall Council.

Discovered Passion for Design | Fall, 2011
I started designing posters and apparel for Phi Sigma Pi and was appointed as Public Relations Chair. I loved coming up with creative ways to promote our events, recruitment activities, and fundraisers.

Started Freelancing | Winter, 2012
After posting some of my designs online, I started getting offers to do freelance design work. I began considering a career in design and advertising.

Joined SB Media Group | Spring, 2012
As a Graphic Designer at SBMG, I worked on campaigns for a local business, a local non-profit, and a tech start-up. I loved designing for these innovative, inspiring, and community-driven clients.

Graduated & Got A Job | Spring, 2013
After graduating from UCSB with an Honors degree in Psychology, I started working as a Designer at Renuf Digital Agency and had the amazing opportunity to design for clients like ASUS Beijing.

Started Grad School | Fall, 2013
After a few months, I left my job to pursue my Master's degree in Visual Communication Design.

2014
